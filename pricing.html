Pricing FAQ

Common questions about plans and billing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access to ambient listening, SOAP notes and ICD suggestions.

How is Enterprise pricing calculated?

Enterprise plans are typically priced on AI minutes processed per month, with volume discounts for larger deployments.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, plans can be upgraded or adjusted at any time as your organization's usage changes.

Do you offer discounts for medical transcription or coding partners?

Yes. Partner and volume pricing is available for transcription companies, coding companies and RCM organizations — reach out to our partnerships team.
