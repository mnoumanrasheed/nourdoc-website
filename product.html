Platform

One ambient layer across the entire clinical encounter.

From the first word spoken in the exam room to a coded, EMR-ready record, NourDoc handles the full documentation lifecycle — built for individual physicians and enterprise health systems alike.

Ambient Listening

Documentation that starts the moment the visit does.

NourDoc listens to the natural conversation between physician and patient — no dictation, no templates, no interruption to the visit. Consent prompts and recording indicators are built into every session.

  • • Works on desktop, tablet and mobile in the exam room
  • • Multi-speaker separation for physician, patient and family
  • • Ambient mode or manual recording, by clinician preference
Core Capabilities

Everything documentation requires, generated automatically.

Medical Transcription

Clinical-grade speech recognition trained on medical terminology, accents and specialty vocabulary.

SOAP Notes

Structured Subjective, Objective, Assessment and Plan notes drafted directly from the conversation.

Clinical Summary

A concise, physician-ready summary of the encounter for quick review before sign-off.

ICD Suggestions

Coding-ready ICD suggestions grounded in what was actually documented during the visit.

Referral Letters

Specialist referral letters generated from the relevant portion of the encounter, ready to send.

Discharge Summary

Complete discharge documentation compiled automatically at the close of an inpatient stay.

Prescription Assistance

Medication details mentioned in conversation are captured and structured for physician review.

EMR Integration

Structured output delivered directly into your existing EMR or HMIS — no duplicate entry.

Batch Audio Processing

Upload recorded encounters in bulk for transcription and structuring at scale.

Hospital Dashboard

Enterprise visibility into documentation turnaround, coding readiness and physician usage across departments.

API & Developer Integration

Future AI Agents

A roadmap toward task-executing clinical agents — order entry, follow-up scheduling and care coordination.
